Atal Tinkering Labs

Aligning with the vision of Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) by NITI Aayog, Nimbus Innovations works closely with institutions to strengthen and effectively utilize Atal Tinkering Labs — ensuring they become active centers of innovation, creativity, and hands-on learning.

Our ATL Support

Two distinct models
for two different needs.

Whether your school already has an ATL or is planning to establish one, we provide structured, end-to-end support aligned with AIM's vision.

Supporting Existing ATL Labs

For institutions that already have an ATL setup, we focus on maximizing its potential and ensuring it remains an active, productive environment.

We Help You
  • Improve utilization of existing lab infrastructure
  • Conduct structured innovation sessions for students
  • Encourage hands-on learning and systematic experimentation
  • Build a strong culture of creativity and problem-solving
Operational Support
  • Continuous supply of essential lab components — sensors, development boards, modules, and consumables
  • Support in maintaining an organized and efficient lab environment
  • Ensuring the lab remains consistently active and productive

Setting Up Innovation Labs

For institutions planning to establish their own innovation lab or ATL, we provide complete end-to-end support — from initial design through to ongoing operations.

We Assist With
  • Lab design and setup tailored to institutional needs and available space
  • Installation of required tools and systems — electronics, fabrication tools, and more
  • Structured implementation and usage planning for the lab
  • Continuous operational support and maintenance
  • Regular supply of consumable materials for ongoing activities
Our Focus

Not just setting up the lab — ensuring it is actively used and delivers real, measurable impact for students.

Why This Matters

What Atal Tinkering Labs
are meant to achieve.

ATLs were established under Atal Innovation Mission to foster innovation at the school level. With the right guidance and execution, these labs can meaningfully transform student learning.

🔬

Transform How Students Learn

Move students from passive consumption of knowledge to active creation — building, testing, and refining ideas in a structured environment.

💡

Encourage Experimentation

Create a safe, structured space where students feel empowered to try new ideas, make mistakes, and learn from them.

🛠️

Build Practical Skills Early

Students who develop hands-on skills at the school level are significantly better prepared for higher education and professional environments.

🌱

Foundation for Future Innovators

Early exposure to innovation tools and methodologies creates a strong foundation for the next generation of India's problem-solvers.

"Enabling institutions to fully realize the vision of innovation-driven education."
